## Controls
* W - CAMERA IN
* S - CAMERA OUT
* A  - CAMERA LEFT
* D  - CAMERA RIGHT
* Move MouseCursor - Rotation in Pitch
* Scroll up - ZOOM IN
* Scroll Down - ZOOM OUT
 ## Dependencies
* GLFW
* GLM
* STB
